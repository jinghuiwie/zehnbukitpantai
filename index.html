About Zehn Bukit Pantai

Zehn Bukit Pantai is a leasehold condominium nestled in Bukit Pantai, Kuala Lumpur. The name "Zehn" is originated from the German word for number ten. It is situated right next to Pantai Medical Centre, Bangsar. Full condominium facilities are built including Jacuzzi, 50-meter lap pool, sauna, gymnasium, squash courts and multi-purpose hall to name a few. One of Zehn Bukit Pantai main attractions is its state-of-art kitchen, designed by internationally renowned chef, Cheong Liew. Nestled in the affluent address of Bangsar, Zehn Bukit Pantai is surrounded by ample amenities to serve the residents with The Gardens and Mid Valley Megamall located just 5 minutes away from the condominium. Bangsar Village and Bangsar Shopping Centre are also within close proximity.
